ANNUAL PROGRAMMES

CSP Olympiad

CSP Olympiad is competitive exam with questions related to Computer Science and Programming. The main aim of this program is to provide the students with the opportunity to showcase their talent and knowledge about Computer and Codes. Any students of SXC, Maithighar can participate in this competition. Each participant will be awarded with the certificate of participation’ and winners will be awarded with certificate, medal and cash prices.

Website Development Competition

Website Development Competition is competition that focuses on Development and design. The main aim of this program is to provide the students with the opportunity to showcase their web designing and coding skills. Any students of SXC, Maithighar can participate in this competition. Each participant will be awarded with the certificate of participation’ and winners will be awarded with certificate, medal and cash prices. Top five websites will be hosted for free with .com domain.

SX3C’s Computer Festival

SX3C’s Computer Festival is the main event organized by SX3C. This is going to be the competitive event where students will compete presenting their works and ideas related to Computer Science, Coding, and Technology. Top 3 presenters will be filtered at different level. And finally, the Final Festival will be organized with these 3 presentations. The Final Festival is to be organized with Fathers, Sisters, Guests, Teachers and Students. The top three presentations along with few other show (dances, songs, poems) will be the content of the program.
