Guide

It is very easy to play this game.
Once you start the game, simply the apples will fall down and you have to catch the apples moving the bucket right or left. You should use the arrows in the keyboard to move the bucket. If you cathch one apple your score will increase by 5. If you miss one, your chance to miss apples decreases by 1. Good luck for your highest score...
